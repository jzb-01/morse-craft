MORSE-CRAFT

Learn, practice, translate, and explore the language of dots and dashes.

Morse code is a communication system that represents letters, numbers, and symbols through combinations of short and long signals. Developed during the early age of telegraphy, it allowed messages to travel across vast distances using nothing more than rhythm and timing.

Though modern technology has largely replaced it, Morse remains one of the most recognizable communication systems ever created. It continues to be used by radio operators, enthusiasts, historians, and anyone interested in understanding how information can be reduced to its simplest form.
