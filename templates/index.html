MORSE-CRAFT

Learn, practice, translate, and explore the language of dots and dashes.

Morse code is a communication system that represents letters, numbers, and symbols through combinations of short and long signals. Developed during the age of telegraphy, it allowed messages to travel across vast distances using nothing more than rhythm, timing, and electrical pulses.

Long before instant messaging and wireless networks, Morse connected cities, ships, railways, and people around the world. Its elegant simplicity continues to fascinate radio operators, historians, and learners who want to understand one of the most influential communication systems ever created.
